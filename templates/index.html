Paris, France

Known as the "City of Love," Paris is famous for its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. The city is renowned for its art, culture, and delicious cuisine.

Rome, Italy

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city rich in history and architecture. The Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City with St. Peter's Basilica are must-visit attractions for history enthusiasts.

Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto is a city in Japan known for its well-preserved temples, traditional wooden houses, and beautiful gardens. It offers a glimpse into Japan's ancient culture, with landmarks like the Golden Pavilion (Kinkaku-ji) and Fushimi Inari Shrine.

Sydney, Australia

Sydney is a vibrant city known for the Sydney Opera House, Sydney Harbour Bridge, and beautiful beaches like Bondi Beach. The city offers a perfect blend of urban and natural attractions.
